Auto-Generate a Presentation using Your Text & Style

We do not store your content. It is used in-memory to plan slides.

Paste only the token — no “Bearer ”, no spaces. Keys aren’t stored.

We will inherit layouts, fonts, and colors from your file and reuse its images.

How it works

  1. We ask your chosen LLM to map your text into a concise slide plan.
  2. We open your PPT template to inherit styles, layouts, and theme colors.
  3. We render slides with titles and bullets, and optionally speaker notes.
  4. We reuse images present in your template for tasteful accents.

No AI image generation is used. Keys and text are not stored.

Tips

  • Add a one-line guidance like “sales deck”, “research summary”, or “visual-heavy”.
  • Use a clean template with clear Title/Content layouts.
  • You can deploy this app to Render/Railway in minutes.
